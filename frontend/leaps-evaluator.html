Where to find setups

RSI Screen

Finviz: RSI(14) under 35 on daily. Thinkorswim: custom scan for RSI < 30 on weekly. Cross-check both timeframes for alignment.

52-Week Lows + Prior Base

Stocks down 30–60% YTD retesting multi-year support. Look for failed breakdowns and base patterns on the weekly chart.

IV Rank < 30

Market Chameleon or Thinkorswim IV Rank scan. Filter for IVR under 30. Avoid buying LEAPS after an earnings IV spike.

Strong Business, Weak Price

High-conviction names in your watchlist that are down 20–40% from highs without a fundamental reason. Sector-wide selloffs often create mispricings.

200 SMA Touches

Scan for stocks touching or piercing the 200-day SMA for the first time in a trend. Especially powerful when RSI is simultaneously oversold.

Liquid Options Filter

After finding a setup, check open interest on the 12–18 month chain. Minimum ~500 OI, bid-ask spread under 1% of mid on a $5+ contract.
